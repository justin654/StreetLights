Before make anything,make sure you have this snap settings(Edit>Snap settings):
X = 2
Y = 1
Z = 2
Scale = 0.1
Rotation = 90

Tips for building:
Drag&Drop your model\prefab in the 'Hierarchy' menu,this will placed your object in center of scene with perfectly zero coordinates!
When you do any manipulation with object(move/rotation),hold "Ctrl" to use 'Grid snapping'.
